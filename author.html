Who Am I?

I am a photographer who absolute loves her hobby and everything that comes with it. I appreciate the people I meet, the places I go, the hours I keep and the spontaneity in every shoot.

I appreciate the raw reality of everyday life. I do my very best to take every picture perfectly. I want my viewers to relate to my photographs through recognition of and familiarity with the situations, the emotions, and energy so much that they wonder if they are looking at a picture of someone they know.
